What is HackForVenezuela?


An apolitical environment to support the free flow of ideas


A learning opportunity
NO PRIOR KNOWLEDGE OR TECHNICAL EXPERIENCE REQUIRED!


A developing network of diverse individuals with a drive to help


Dedicated time to develop software solutions for Venezuelan community

You can contribute!

Want to make an app but don't know how?

Attend our Mendix workshop and learn to make an app without coding, or attend our Android workshop and learn to program an app using Java!


Don't want to make an app?

GHA will be hosting talks, workshops, and activities thorughout the day so you can find a way to contribute that fits your skills and interests
